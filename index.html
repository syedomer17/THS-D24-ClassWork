Code for India Foundation is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to delivering high-quality, industry-focused technology programs for youth from marginalized backgrounds.

Dismantling the socio-economic barriers that prevent the participation of underrepresented and underprivileged through free and equal access to coding education programs designed under the Code For India initiative, despite their economic circumstances. Hence, inclining them to opt for a better and improved quality of life.
